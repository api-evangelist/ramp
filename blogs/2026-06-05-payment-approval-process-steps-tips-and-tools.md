The payment approval process is the sequence of steps organizations use to authenticate, authorize, and schedule vendor payments before funds are released, functioning as a safeguard between invoice receipt and disbursement. An inadequate approval framework creates vulnerability to duplicate payments and fraud, while a robust system protects financial performance, sustains vendor relationships, and produces clear audit documentation. The article outlines the workflow steps finance teams should implement to ensure accuracy, compliance, and budgetary alignment before processing any transaction.
