Startup financial planning combines revenue projections, expense budgeting, and cash flow management into a practical roadmap for growth, helping founders manage uncertainty while building credibility with investors and teams. Unlike established companies with historical data to rely on, startups must plan amid uncertainty, making a clear view of runway and trade-offs essential. A strong financial plan turns uncertainty into something manageable by revealing cash position, milestone funding needs, and the trade-offs available at each stage.
