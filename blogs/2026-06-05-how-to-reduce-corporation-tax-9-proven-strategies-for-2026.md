Ramp's Head of Accounting Partner Channel outlines nine legal strategies for reducing corporate tax liability, including maximizing deductions, claiming available credits, timing income and expenses strategically, and choosing the right business entity structure. The article targets both fast-growing startups and established mid-market companies looking to preserve cash by lowering taxable net profit. Reducing taxable income through deductions is framed as the primary legitimate pathway to a lower tax bill.
